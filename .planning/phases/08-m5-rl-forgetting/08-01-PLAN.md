<objective>
Implement the M5 RL forgetting model: JAX likelihood functions with phi_rl parameter, MLE parameter registration, and full fitting pipeline integration.

Purpose: M5 extends M3 by adding global per-trial Q-value decay toward baseline (Q0=1/3) BEFORE the delta-rule update. This dissociates RL forgetting from WM decay (phi), enabling the model to capture participants who forget learned Q-values between trials.

Output: Three files modified such that `python scripts/12_fit_mle.py --model wmrl_m5` runs end-to-end and phi_rl=0 reduces exactly to M3.
</objective>
