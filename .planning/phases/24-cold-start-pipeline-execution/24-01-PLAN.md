    **Operator briefing — failure-recovery policy (CONTEXT.md Area 2, RETRY IS NOT A FRAMING):**

    Over the next 24-48h, three failure classes may arise. None are called "retries":

    1. **SLURM timeout / OOM / node eviction** (State=TIMEOUT or FAILED with OOM message): this is a **resource-allocation bug**, not a retry. Procedure:
       - Bump `--time=` or `--mem=` in the specific SLURM file.
       - Commit the change (single atomic commit).
       - Resubmit ONLY the failed step + afterok-chain the downstream steps via `--dependency=afterok:<new_jid>`.
       - Same seed. Fresh JobID.
       - Wave 2 audit log: one-line operator note; NO "retry chain" table.

    2. **MCMC diagnostic failure on completed job** (State=COMPLETED, ExitCode=0:0, but R-hat > 1.05 / divergences > 0): this is **not a SLURM failure at all**. Procedure:
       - LOG-AND-CONTINUE. Do NOT halt the chain.
       - The stage 06 loo_stacking step filters non-convergent models from the stacking pool (CONTEXT.md Area 3: "Non-convergent models EXCLUDED from LOO+stacking pool").
       - No retry, no re-seed, no re-prior.

    3. **Mid-chain SLURM failure that cascades via afterok** (downstream jobs CANCELLED): the scheduler propagates the failure. Operator:
       - Identifies the failing step from sacct.
       - Follows protocol (1) for resource failures.
       - Re-chains downstream via `submit_all.sh --from-stage <N>` after the resubmitted step completes.

    **Mid-chain seeds NEVER change.** A same-seed resubmission after node eviction is "the same run, continued" — byte-identical to the hypothetical uninterrupted run (important for Phase 25's reproducibility regression).

    **Conservative resource padding already applied in v4.0 SLURMs:** `--time=` ≈ 1.5× max observed v4.0 wall; `--mem=` ≈ 1.5× peak MaxRSS. Trades queue time for one-shot success.

    **Auto-push commits during run:** ~30 commits land on `main` between submission and terminus. DO NOT manually `git add` artifacts. Expected commit messages: `bayes_<stage>_<job> job <JID> results`.

    **Per-step log paths** (for `tail -f` during monitoring):
    - Stage 03: `logs/bayesian_03_prefit_<JID>.{out,err}`
    - Stage 04b: `logs/bayesian_04b_<MODEL>_<JID>.{out,err}`
    - Stage 04c (L2 dispatcher): `logs/21_dispatch_l2_<JID>.{out,err}`; children: `logs/bayesian_04c_<MODEL>_<JID>.{out,err}`
    - Stage 05: `logs/post_checks_<STEP>_<JID>.{out,err}`
    - Stage 06: `logs/fit_analyses_<STEP>_<JID>.{out,err}`
